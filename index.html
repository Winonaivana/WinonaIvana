Hi! I'm

I'm an illustrator and visual storyteller, combining traditional art
with modern illustrations to create new worlds and perspectives. When
I'm not drawing, you can usually find me out in nature.
I am lucky to work from home, while enjoying my family.

I am inspired by the beauty that surrounds me and the fairy tales
that I write in my mind since I was a child. My work reflects my childhood
of cartoons and my dog exploring nature. My favorite techniques are
watercolors, colored pencils, and of course digital art on the iPad.
